## üí° Tips for Success

### 1. **Struggle is Learning**
- Spend 15-20 min struggling before asking for hints
- The struggle builds neural pathways

### 2. **Spaced Repetition is Key**
- Review problems after 1 day, 1 week, 1 month
- This is how you build long-term memory

### 3. **Teach to Learn**
- Explain solutions out loud (to me or yourself)
- If you can't explain it, you don't understand it

### 4. **Quality Over Speed**
- Better to deeply understand 50 problems than rush through 77
- Adjust timeline if needed

### 5. **Track Patterns, Not Just Problems**
- After each problem, note: "This is a [pattern] problem because..."
- Build your pattern recognition muscle
